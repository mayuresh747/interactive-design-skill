Core Differentiator

A skill that gets smarter every time you use it

Most tools ship with a fixed set of templates that never change. This skill learns new patterns from every site it analyzes and adds them to its library automatically.

Point it at a site with a technique it hasn't seen — a novel scroll behavior, an unusual hero layout, a creative hover effect — and it will reverse-engineer, catalog, and reuse it in future builds.

It starts with 38 patterns. After a few projects, it could be 50, 60, or more. Your skill becomes uniquely yours.
